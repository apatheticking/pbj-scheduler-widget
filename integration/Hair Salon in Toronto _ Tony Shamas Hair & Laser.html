Ben's Hair Salon – Toronto

Pamper yourself with a visit to Ben's Hair & Laser, located at 81 Jarvis St on the North East corner of Jarvis St and Adelaide St. in the heart of downtown Toronto. Rely on our team of professionally trained stylists, colour specialists and laser experts to provide you with the look you want – or the look you’ve always wanted – in a sumptuously serene salon environment that’s a world away from the bustle of the city outside.

Not only haircuts and styles, but atmosphere, music and customer service all play an important role in the experience we aim to create for our guests. Stimulate your senses and mind in a place that diversifies itself from the commons hair salons in the city.

To schedule your appointment or to arrange for a free consultation at the best hair salon in Toronto, call 416-941-9915 or book online today.

About Us

A Hair Salon that doesn’t feel like a salon: welcome home.
Come home, to a relaxing gem in the heart of downtown Toronto's Old Town.

With our friendly, generous, professional stylists and associates, come home to fashion, elegance, and simplicity.

Atmosphere as the centre of a concept of lifestyle born in an ancient world where style, culture and creation first met.

Commitment to the best costumer experience is our passion.

We love what we do.

The concept of Tony Shamas hair & laser is very simple yet sophisticated. Guests have to feel the most comfortable just like when they are relaxing at home. With the luxury of beauty and pampering.

A cozy, elegant and energetic atmosphere surrounds you while enjoying a foamy cappuccino, made with Professional Coffee Machine FAEMA from Italy - just like they serve it at coffee places there - or choose from a multiple varieties of selected fine and delicate teas.

Mystical scents float in the air emanating from weekly-selected candles from the best fragrance-making European houses. While the smocks wrapped around guests are infused with Hand-Made Diptyque Ceramic Ovals from Paris.
